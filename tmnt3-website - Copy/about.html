Plot Summary

In *Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles III*, the Turtles are transported back in time to feudal Japan. They must save their friend April O'Neil and battle the evil samurai warlord, Walker. Together, they fight for justice, honor, and to return to their time.

Fun Facts:

  • This was the third movie in the TMNT film series.
  • The Turtles time travel through a magical scepter.
  • The movie features a mix of practical effects and animatronics for the Turtles themselves.
