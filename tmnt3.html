Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles III (1993)

Overview

In the third installment, the Turtles travel back in time to feudal Japan to rescue their friend April O'Neil, who has been sent back in time by an ancient Japanese artifact.

Plot

The Turtles are transported back to Japan where they must fight in the past while trying to find a way home before they are trapped forever.

Main Characters

  • April O'Neil - The Turtles' reporter friend.
  • The Ancient Japanese Warriors - Allies in their time travel adventure.
