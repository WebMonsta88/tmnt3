Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles II: The Secret of the Ooze (1991)

Overview

Released in 1991, "The Secret of the Ooze" is the second film in the TMNT franchise. Directed by Michael Pressman, this sequel follows the Turtles as they battle against new foes while uncovering the secrets behind the mysterious ooze that created them.

Plot

The Turtles face off against the mutated henchmen Tokka and Rahzar, created from the mysterious green ooze that gave the Turtles their powers. They must also deal with the return of their arch-nemesis, The Shredder, who seeks to harness the power of the ooze for his own evil purposes.

Characters

Main Characters

  • The Turtles: As before, the four brothers continue their fight for justice.
  • Shredder: The main antagonist who plans to recreate the ooze to create more mutants to serve him.
  • Tokka & Rahzar: Mutant creatures who were created from the ooze to fight the Turtles.
