Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles II: The Secret of the Ooze (1991)

Overview

The sequel to the original film, TMNT 2 introduces new characters and amps up the action, featuring the introduction of the Turtles' greatest foes: the Foot Clan and the powerful Super Shredder!

Plot

Following the events of the first movie, the Turtles must face new threats as they discover the mysterious “Ooze” that gave them their powers.

Main Characters

  • Shredder - The Turtles' arch-nemesis.
  • Tokka and Rahzar - Mutant creatures created by the Ooze.
