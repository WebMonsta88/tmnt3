Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles (1990)

Overview

The first live-action TMNT movie introduced the world to Leonardo, Donatello, Michelangelo, and Raphael. Directed by Steve Barron, the film was a major success and helped cement the franchise as a pop culture phenomenon.

Plot

Four pizza-loving turtles, raised in the sewers of New York City, fight to protect the city from evil forces, led by the villainous Shredder.

Main Characters

  • Leonardo - The leader and disciplined turtle.
  • Donatello - The tech genius and inventor.
  • Michelangelo - The easygoing, pizza-loving turtle.
  • Raphael - The tough, hot-headed rebel.
